DeepDream AI

DeepDream AI is a website that embedded with artwork generator which aim to commercialise the autism people artwork by utilising the Artificial Intelligence (AI) technique. Unique artwork is created in collaboration between autism people and machine .

About us

Different, Not Less — Dr Temple Grandin

Concept of DeepDream AI

Many autism people faced difficulty in maintaining thier life due to lack of support from the social. We provide a platform for them to publish thier art to generate fund.

Why DeepDream AI?

DeepDream AI is free for autism people and public to use !!. In addition, various service are provided such as Printing service, Augmented Reality (AR), Chatbot, Marketplace, etc.

Our Main Objective

DeepDream AI's main objective is to generate necessary funds to support kids and family with special needs, by selling the autism people's artwork in the marketplace.

>

What Are The Services We offer


Printing Services

Having a physical copy of your artwork to display in your place is certainly satisfying! In collabaration with our partner printing service lab, our client may print out whatever arts they like at ease, all of which preserve original colors and offer a slew of gorgeous arts.

Online Marketplace

We have prepared an online platform with a delightful web experience for the client. Various features are developed to enrich client experience such as review forum, logistics, payment gateway, etc.

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality allow client to map the artwork with frame to real world place through the lens of phone. We provide augmented reality services as a trial preview to let client visualize the effect of the artworks at certain location especially on an empty wall before buying the artwork.

Chatbot

The chatbot is developed to process human conversation and given response to them. Client can seek for help if they face difficulty in using our web application. In addition, client can ask question about our product or return policy or term and condition, etc.
